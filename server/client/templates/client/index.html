ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Prottolk : Enhancing Industrial Efficiency and Safety with Automation Solutions.

Prottolk is a total turnkey solution supplier aimed at improving the efficiency and safety of industrial plants through plant automation. The company is able to supply, install, and commission projects.

The company offers after sales support for new and existing automation installations, through preventative maintenance, specialized customer training and technical support for Variable Speed Drives (VSD’s), Programmable Logic Circuits (PLC’s), Solar Inverters and Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) Switchgears.

Prottolk offers complete industrial plant automation solutions to improve efficiency and safety. We handle supply, installation, commissioning, and provide after-sales support for VSDs, PLCs, Solar Inverters, and LV/MV Switchgears. Our expertise spans multiple industries including Mining, Power Generation, Solar Energy, FMCG, and Water.

Using valuable experience gained in Service engineering, Sales, and Product Management, Prottolk can provide technical and commercial solution-based assistance in the Mining, Power Generation, Solar Energy, FMCG, and Water Industries.
